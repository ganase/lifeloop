lifeloop is a local-first habit notification app provided by Knock Knock 株式会社. It does not require sign-in and does not upload location, habit, or log data to a backend server.

Location permission is used to register places, show the user's current location on the in-app map, and trigger local notifications when the device enters or exits registered regions. Notification permission is used only for local notifications.

The app is not intended for emergency, medical, medication, driving, or safety-critical use. Local notifications may be delayed or missed depending on iOS location services, device settings, battery state, and region monitoring behavior.

To test the notification flow without physically moving:
1. Launch the app.
2. Allow location and notification permissions.
3. Open the Place tab.
4. Swipe left on a place row and tap "テスト".
5. A local notification is delivered.
6. Open the notification to confirm that the app navigates to the Log screen.

No demo account is required because the app has no account or login feature.
